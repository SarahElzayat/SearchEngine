10 Ham Sandwiches That Are Anything But Ordinary

By Hayley Sugg August 09, 2021
Skip gallery slides
Pin
Korean Street Toast on a grey plate
Credit: Chef John

If you're a fan of ham sandwiches, but bored with the same cheese and mustard routine, then try these 10 unique ham sandwich recipes to change things up. Get spicy with a green chile grilled ham and cheese, start the day with a hammy egg maple bagel, or go international with super savory Korean street toast. You'll never go back to run-of-the-mill ham sandwiches after a single bite of these wow-worthy ham sandwich ideas.

Spicy Ham and Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Spicy Ham and Grilled Cheese Sandwich on a white plate
Credit: *Sherri*
View Recipe this link opens in a new tab

Your standard ham-and-cheese melt is upgraded with thinly sliced green chiles for a kick of heat and toasty rye bread for a tangy taste. No green chiles on hand? Reviewers have had success subbing in jalapeños or serrano peppers, but scale back if a person with a sensitive heat palate will be eating. Those peppers can be much hotter than green chiles.
